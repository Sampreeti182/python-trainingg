COMPARE BETWEEN ON_PREMISES DEPLOYMENT VS CLOUD DEPLOYMENT


On-Premises Deployment

Definition: All hardware, software, and data are hosted within the organizationâ€™s physical location.
Key Characteristics:

Full control over infrastructure.
Requires large upfront investment in hardware and software.
Maintenance and upgrades handled by in-house IT team.


Pros:

High security and compliance control.
Customization according to business needs.


Cons:

Expensive to scale.
Limited flexibility and remote accessibility.
Requires dedicated IT staff.



 Cloud Deployment

Definition: Infrastructure and services are hosted by a third-party provider and accessed via the internet.
Key Characteristics:

Pay-as-you-go pricing.
Managed by cloud provider (maintenance, updates, security).
Accessible from anywhere.


Pros:

Cost-effective and scalable.
Quick deployment and innovation.
Disaster recovery and backup included.


Cons:

Dependency on internet connectivity.
Shared infrastructure may raise security concerns.
Limited customization compared to on-prem.
